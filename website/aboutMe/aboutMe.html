I am a senior at Greenwich Academy in Conneticut. I live with my two brothers, mom, dad, and dogs.

I was born in 2004 and have lived in Greenwich my entire life. I look forward to starting college and the countless new experiences I will have in the next four years and beyond. I love to travel and meet new people, so nothing is more exciting to me than the prospect of doing so in an academic context. I hope to study abroad to grow my language skills and cultural awareness.

I also enjoy a lot of familiar things. I love to immerse myself in natures and conversations. I find peace in music. My favorite food is sushi, however, I am not the best when it comes to rolling maki. I am better at cooking other foods, with my current favorite food to make being pulled pork. Academic topics that captivate me include computer science, creative writing, economics, 19th and 20th century European literature, calculus, and more.

I have played piano for over a decade, and currently my favorite song to play is "Waltz in A Minor" by Frederic Chopin. I love to hang out with friends and enjoy time with family. My favorite places on earth are Casco, Maine and Providence, Rhode Island where my mom and grandparents grew up. I created this website as an archive of myself and as a way to learn more about me. I hope you enjoy!

Click to see a video of me playing "Valse Sentimentale" by Franz Schubert.
I love this song, and played it in a recital prior to COVID-19. What I love about classical
piano is that I feel it throughout my body. To me, this song is very bittersweet.
Through the dimuendos and crescendos I experience the love story of my grandparents, with the final
notes reminding me of my grandmother's last days. Nothing makes me feel like classical
piano does. To play with your emotions is to play like the greats; when I began feeling
my emotions in every key, my skills heightened. I hope you enjoy this song.
